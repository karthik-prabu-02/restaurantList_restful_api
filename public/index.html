Indian Butter Chicken

Indian butter chicken, also known as "Murgh Makhani," is a popular and flavorful dish in Indian cuisine. It is made with tender chicken pieces that are marinated in a mixture of yogurt and spices, then cooked in a creamy tomato-based sauce. The sauce is rich and buttery, with a blend of spices that gives it a distinctive flavor.
